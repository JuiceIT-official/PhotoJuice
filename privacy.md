Privacy Policy PhotoJuice

Scope
This privacy policy applies to the iOS app PhotoJuice, published by DATATIDE GmbH, Heinrich‑Hertz‑Str. 7, 90552 Röthenbach.
Processed Data
Images
All image data is processed exclusively on the user’s device. The app is solely intended to select, sort and delete images.
No usage metrics
No usage statistics, analytics or telemetry data are collected, transmitted or stored by us or any third party.
Data Transfer
There is no transfer of images or other content to the cloud or external servers.
Permissions
Required access
The app requests only the permission necessary to select, view and delete images: Photos access. This permission is used solely to enable the app’s sorting and deletion functionality.
Revocation
You may revoke this permission at any time in the iOS settings. If access is revoked, the sorting and deletion features will not function until access is restored.
Security and Deletion
All processed data remains on your device. Deleted images are removed according to iOS mechanisms. Uninstalling the app removes local app data. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect local data from unauthorized access. Physical security of the device is the responsibility of the device owner.
GDPR Compliance and Contact
We strictly comply with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Processing of image data is limited to what is necessary for the app’s functionality and, where applicable, is based on your consent or the performance of the service you request.
Your rights
You have the rights to access, rectify, erase, restrict processing, and data portability, and you may withdraw consent at any time. To exercise your rights or for questions, contact: info@datatide.de.
Complaints
If you believe your data is processed in violation of the GDPR, you may lodge a complaint with the competent supervisory authority.
