Processed Data
Images
All image data is processed exclusively on the user’s device. The app is solely intended to select, sort and delete images.
No usage metrics
No usage statistics, analytics or telemetry data are collected, transmitted, or stored by us or any third party.

Data Transfer and Security
No transfer of images or other content to the cloud or external servers takes place.
Security
We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect local data from unauthorized access. The physical security of the device is the responsibility of the device owner.
---
Permissions and Deletion
Required access
The app requests only the permission necessary to select, view and delete images: Photos access. This permission is used solely to enable the app’s sorting and deletion functionality.
Revocation
You may revoke this permission at any time in the iOS settings. If access is revoked, the sorting and deletion features will not function until access is restored.
Deletion
All processed data remains on your device. Deleted images are removed according to iOS mechanisms. Uninstalling the app removes local app data.
